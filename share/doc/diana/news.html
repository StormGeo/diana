News version 3.22.3
  • Vertical profiles Temp and pilot from bufr, amdar from obs-files
  • Observations Data sources are bufr-files by default, data form obs-files are still available as "synop-obs" etc.
    • Precipitation from synop-bufr: New parameters RRR_1, RRR_6, RRR_12 and RRR_24 available in list plot.
      RRR :
      At 06 and 18: precipitation past 12 hours
      At 00 and 12: precipitation past 6 hours
      Else precipitation past 1 hour
      (In datatype synop-obs, accumulation time is not well defined)
    • Wind gust:
      At 00, 06, 12 and 18: max wind gust past 6 hours
      At 03, 09, 15 and 21: max wind gust past 3 hours
      Else wind gust past 1 hour
    • Max wind:
      At 00, 06, 12 and 18: max wind gust past 6 hours
      At 03, 09, 15 and 21: max wind gust past 3 hours
    • Aireps:
      Moved from plottype "Pressure" to plottype "Synop/List"
